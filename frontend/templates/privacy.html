Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Quikbok ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our AI booking platform, in compliance with the Indian Information Technology Act, 2000 and its associated rules.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Name and contact details (email, phone number)
  • Business information (business name, type, address)
  • WhatsApp numbers for booking notifications
  • Payment information (processed through secure third-party gateways)

2.2 Booking Data

  • Customer booking requests and preferences
  • Service details and scheduling information
  • Communication logs with AI assistant

2.3 Technical Data

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our AI booking services
  • To process bookings and send notifications via WhatsApp
  • To communicate with customers regarding their bookings
  • To improve our services and develop new features
  • To ensure security and prevent fraudulent activities
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Supabase

We use Supabase as our database provider. Your data is stored securely on their servers, which comply with international data protection standards.

4.2 Twilio

We use Twilio for WhatsApp messaging services. Your phone numbers and message content are processed through Twilio's secure infrastructure to send booking notifications and AI responses.

4.3 Razorpay

We use Razorpay for payment processing. Your payment information is handled directly by Razorpay's secure payment gateway and is not stored on our servers.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Secure coding practices and regular updates

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your personal data by contacting us at support@quikbok.com.

7. Your Rights Under Indian IT Act

Under the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is being collected
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Consent to the collection of sensitive personal data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Request deletion of personal information

8. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience and analyze website traffic. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
